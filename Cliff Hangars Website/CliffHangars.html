Welcome to one of the best climbing schools in America, located in one of the best climbing areas in America – the Colorado Rockies. Nowhere else is there such a great variety of technically challenging rock, and ice. We present the climber with a choice of multiple-pitch climbs and big wall routes. Whether you are here to enjoy the spectacular scenery or just to train for an expedition, you can choose no better place than Colorado.


And no one else brings the experience of the Colorado Rockies to you better than Cliff Hangers Climbing School. Whether you are a beginner itching to learn the shill and art of climbing, or an experienced mountaineer looking for guides to take you on some of Rocky Mountain National Park’s more challenging climbs, consider Cliff Hangers Climbing School your gateway to spectacular mountains.
